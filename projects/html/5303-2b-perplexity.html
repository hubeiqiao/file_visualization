Research Process

Based on Assignment 2A, we compiled a list of 66 PQC companies and gathered a corpus of their products and solutions. We combined this data into a CSV file for analysis using Orange.

1.1 Set up Orange workflow

1.2 Load data

Load the corpus from 1A into Orange by CSV File Import widget.

1.3 Preprocess data

Transformation:
Lowercase
Tokenization:
Regexp
Filtering:
  • Stopwords - Filtered by stopwords specifying language as english, attached a list of stop words that was built iteratively by identifying common words, company names, product names and domain-specific words that are not meaningful for the objective.
  • Stemming - Lemmagen Lemmatizer English
  • Document Frequency - Relative 0.11 - 0.85 range to remove overly common words and noise

1.4 Topic modeling

Observe key words in different numbers of topic, examine the stability and the replicability of the topic modeling results while considering scores of log perflexity and topic coherence.

Log perplexity

Usually a lower score suggests a better fit of the model. Our result is 32.44154.

Topic coherence

Usually a higher score suggests better topics. Our result is 0.51707.

Identified Topic Models

After processing the data, we identified five distinct topic models:

Topic 1

blockchain cloud design service partner fip storage api ip developer
Secure Digital Infrastructure

This category highlights products built for modern digital environments. They integrate blockchain and cloud technologies with robust APIs and storage solutions, support secure design and development, and adhere to compliance standards (e.g., FIP) to facilitate collaboration among developers and partners.

Topic 2

embed offer discovery bit design advance government method service tool
Embedded Advanced Security

Focused on integration, these solutions are designed to be embedded within hardware or software systems. They offer advanced cryptographic methods and discovery tools with a particular emphasis on government-grade security, enabling innovative design and seamless service delivery.

Topic 3

government encrypt resilient decrypt benefit transmission case problem traditional ability
Resilient Communication & Encryption

Products in this group address the critical need for secure data transmission. They enhance traditional encryption/decryption processes with resilient methods, ensuring that sensitive communications—especially for government use—are reliably protected against emerging threats.

Topic 4

machine visibility full crystal standard offer discover built user advance
Comprehensive Visibility & Analytics

This topic covers tools that deliver full-system visibility through machine-level analytics. With built-in discovery features and adherence to industry standards, these solutions empower users with clear insights and proactive monitoring to maintain robust security.

Topic 5

random protect feature resilient guide authentication state enhance complex decrypt
Robust Authentication & Protection

Emphasizing the fundamentals of secure operations, this category offers features like randomness and advanced decryption to strengthen authentication. These products provide complex, resilient protection mechanisms that guide users in enhancing overall security in challenging environments.

LLM-Based Analysis

Next, we used an LLM to analyze each topic and answer specific questions. We compared different versions and selected the best responses for presentation.

Group 1: Secure Digital Infrastructure

Market Offers & Value Proposition

Companies in this group focus on providing a secure digital foundation that integrates modern technologies such as blockchain and cloud services. Their offerings are built around robust APIs and scalable storage solutions, emphasizing secure design and compliance (e.g., FIP standards) that facilitate collaboration among developers and partners.

Target Customers

Their solutions are particularly attractive to tech-forward enterprises, digital service providers, and developer communities. Organizations looking to build secure, compliant, and interoperable systems in rapidly evolving digital ecosystems are prime targets.

Key Benefits
  • Scalability & Integration: Leveraging cloud and blockchain allows for flexible scaling and seamless integration.
  • Compliance & Security: Adherence to industry standards (like FIP) ensures trust and regulatory compliance.
  • Developer Empowerment: Extensive API support and partner networks make it easier for developers to embed secure functions into their applications.
Differentiators & Resources

Their competitive edge comes from blending cutting‑edge digital technologies with a developer-centric approach. Key resources include a robust cloud infrastructure, blockchain expertise, and a focus on secure design protocols.

Role of AI & Technologies

AI can be integrated to optimize security analytics, automate threat detection, and manage resource allocation across cloud environments—thus enhancing overall system resilience.

Key Cost Drivers

Investments in cloud infrastructure, blockchain integration, and compliance certifications drive costs, along with ongoing R&D to stay ahead in secure design and API development.
