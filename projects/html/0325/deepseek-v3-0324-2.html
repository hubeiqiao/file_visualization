Key Scoping Review Frameworks

Arksey and O'Malley (2005)

Five-step outline that works well for fast-changing fields like fintech. Allows for iterative updates as the review progresses.

Example: Use to broadly map literature on wearable tech for remote patient monitoring, capturing academic journals to patent filings.

Levac et al. (2010)

Extends Arksey and O'Malley's model by emphasizing stakeholder engagementâ€”vital for TIM researchers collaborating with startups and R&D teams.

Example: Engage accelerators and manufacturers to refine scoping review on IoT-based smart manufacturing.

Joanna Briggs Institute (JBI)

Offers refined methodology for planning and conducting scoping reviews, ensuring rigor when examining diverse sources like patents and market analyses.

Example: Follow JBI approach for scoping review on 3D-printing technologies for rapid prototyping.

PRISMA-ScR

Focuses on standardized reporting of scoping reviews, making technology roadmap or innovation policy studies more credible and replicable.

Example: Follow PRISMA-ScR guidelines for transparent reporting on blockchain in supply chains.

9-Step Method to Produce Scoping Reviews

1

Formulate Review Question and Scope

Define research question using frameworks like PCC (Population, Concept, Context). Use AI to generate variations and refine scope.

AI Role Human Oversight
2

Search for Articles

Develop search strategy with Boolean operators. Use AI tools like ChatGPT, Elicit, and Perplexity to refine queries.

AI Role Human Oversight
3

Select Articles

Screen titles/abstracts then full texts using Rayyan AI. Implement dual screening for quality control.

AI Role Human Oversight
4

Extract Data

Use AI tools like Elicit or SciSpace to extract and organize data into tables. Manually verify accuracy.

AI Role Human Oversight
5

Analyze & Synthesize Data

Identify trends, themes, and gaps using AI-powered categorization. Validate AI-generated themes manually.

AI Role Human Oversight
6

Interpret Results

Balance AI-generated insights with critical human evaluation. Identify practical applications for TIM.

AI Role Human Oversight
7

Write the Scoping Review

Use AI for drafting but not finalizing text. Follow PRISMA-ScR guidelines and maintain academic rigor.

AI Role Human Oversight
8

Incorporate Ethical Considerations

Ensure transparency in AI usage, prevent plagiarism, mitigate bias, and comply with ethical guidelines.

AI Role Human Oversight
9

Disseminate Findings

Publish in journals, present at conferences, create policy briefs. Use AI for summarization and visualization.

Recommended AI Tools for Scoping Reviews

ChatGPT

Generates review question variations, suggests Boolean search strings, and summarizes key themes.

Best for: Initial question formulation, search term generation, and preliminary summarization.

Elicit

Extracts key insights from thousands of papers, prioritizes studies, and enables side-by-side comparisons.

Best for: Data extraction, study comparison, and organizing findings into tables.

Perplexity

Retrieves information from academic and non-academic sources, generating answers based on multiple studies.

Best for: Discovering grey literature and connecting insights across diverse sources.

Rayyan AI

Semi-automated screening tool that helps filter and organize studies during the selection process.

Best for: Title/abstract screening, full-text review, and collaborative filtering.

Research Rabbit

Visualizes citation networks to analyze relationships between papers and track influential works.

Best for: Mapping research landscapes and identifying key authors/clusters.

SciSpace

Extracts summaries, conclusions, and findings from multiple PDFs into a tabular format.

Best for: Data extraction from PDFs and organizing literature systematically.

Ethical Considerations in AI-Assisted Reviews

Key Ethical Challenges

  • AI Hallucinations

    AI may invent references or misattribute authors. Always verify citations manually.

  • Algorithmic Bias

    AI may favor Anglophone research or well-funded corporate labs. Diversify sources.

  • Plagiarism Risks

    AI-generated text may resemble existing literature. Use plagiarism detection tools.

Mitigation Strategies

  • Human Oversight

    Critical evaluation of AI outputs by domain experts is essential.

  • Diverse Sources

    Include non-English studies and grey literature to counter bias.

  • Transparency

    Clearly document AI's role in the research process.

AI Transparency and Documentation Checklist

  • Disclose AI-assisted processes

    Clearly state how AI contributed to search, screening, extraction, and synthesis.

  • Validate AI outputs

    Manually check summaries, citations, and interpretations for accuracy.

  • Follow ethical guidelines

    Adhere to institutional policies and professional codes of conduct.

  • Maintain audit trail

    Keep records of AI contributions for reproducibility.
